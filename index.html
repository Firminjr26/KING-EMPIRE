About KING EMPIRE

Your premier destination for exceptional nightclub events

KING EMPIRE is a leading event hosting organization specializing in creating extraordinary nightclub experiences. We bring together top-tier artists like South Africa like ShakesxLes, Uncool Mc, Shera The Dj, Binna Binnz and create unforgettable nights that resonate with music lovers and party enthusiasts.

Our team of experienced event professionals works tirelessly to curate the perfect atmosphere, combining cutting-edge sound systems, spectacular lighting, and world-class talent to deliver experiences that exceed expectations. Over the years, we've proudly hosted signature events such as Balcony Sesh, Z-Fest, Time-Out New Year's Eve, Asante 2023, and The Amapiano Experience, each leaving lasting memories with our audiences.

Our Mission

To revolutionize the nightlife scene by creating immersive, high-energy events that bring people together through the power of music and exceptional entertainment.
